transitioning to full-time RV living november 25th, 2023

It was a joyous occasion, filled with laughter, gratitude, and the realization that our dream of making the RV a functional living space was becoming a reality. Kelly made delicious foods of all thankful varieties and we all had a great night.



following the festivities, Corey and I decided to finally give in to the urge in the backs of our minds since we first bought the RV and spent our first night in the RV on Thanksgiving! The experience was both exciting and cozy, setting the stage for the next phase of our adventure.



upon happily returning home, we took the time to share some love with our cats before diving into the process of seriously moving into the RV. With the power connected, our mobile abode had become livable enough for us to comfortably spend time in!
