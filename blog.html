meet Corey, Michael, the CATS, and the RV!november 17th, 2023



hey there, fellow wanderers! We're Corey and Michael, and we're about to take you on the journey of our lives. We recently became the proud owners of a 1984 Fleetwood Southwind RV, marking the beginning of our nomadic adventure across the gorgous and expansive landscapes of the United States. Buckle up and join us as we traverse the country with our furry companions (click) â€“ Pluto, Romeow, and Phoinix â€“ in tow.



the idea of embarking on this epic road trip has been a dream of ours for a long time, and now, with the wheels of destiny, (and soon the RV,) in motion, we can't wait to share our experiences with you. Our escapades will unfold through social media platforms, including YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok, so you can virtually hop on board and be part of every twist and turn.



but our journey isn't just about the (s)miles we'll cover; it's also about discovering the natural beauty and resources scattered across this diverse land. As we roam from state to state, we plan to harness the power of nature by extracting essential oils from the resources we encounter. These oils will then be transformed into unique fragrances, capturing the essence of each location we visit. From the crisp mountain air to the salty breeze of coastal towns, our fragrances will tell the story of our adventure, and they will be available in limited quantities for sale.



currently, our days are filled with the hum of power tools and the frequent meow of our feline companions as we (admittedly scratch our heads a lot and) work on transforming our RV into a rolling haven. The first order of business? Replacing an alternator belt and addressing a few mechanical "quirks" to ensure our journey is as safe and smooth as possible. Once the mechanical aspects are fine-tuned, our focus will shift to the interior of the RV. Which.. We will get into later.. XD



our to-do list seems abundantly endless, but we're tackling each task with enthusiasm, gratitude, and a shared vision of creating a home on wheels that reflects both functionality and extreme coziness. From designing a space-efficient kitchen to crafting cozy nooks and climbable pieces for our cats, we're pouring our hearts into every detail. We can't wait to reveal the transformation and show you how a vintage RV can be reborn into a luxy and practical mobile home, and on the most budget-est of budgets.

fully clad in rainbows and wrenches, creativity flowing like the leaks we still need to fix, we invite you to follow along as we document the highs, the lows, and everything in between. Whether you're a seasoned traveler, a cat lover, a DIY enthusiast, fragrance lover, or simply seeking a vicarious escape, we hope our journey resonates with you.



So join us as we roll down the scenic routes (at reduced speeds to accomodate our home-on-wheels), chasing sunsets and discovering the hidden gems of America, learning more about people, vehicles, culture, and ourselves along the way. Your front-row seat to our nomadic life awaits, and we can't wait to share it with you. Here's to the open road and the countless adventures that lie ahead!
